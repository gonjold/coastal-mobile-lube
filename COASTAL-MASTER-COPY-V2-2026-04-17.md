## The repositioned core proposition

Everything in the copy now serves one idea:

> **Your vehicle stays put. Your day stays yours.**

Not "we come to you" (table stakes — every competitor says it). Not "convenient" (vague). The specific thing: your day doesn't reroute around vehicle service. The vehicle doesn't reroute around your day. Both stay where they are. We bring the shop.

Three differentiators that back it up:

1. **Dealership-grade vacuum extraction** — clean, fast, no drain plug, no mess at any location
2. **Tire delivery + on-site install** — order tires online, ship to your location, we mount and balance where you are
3. **Multi-vertical from one provider** — cars, boats, RVs, fleets, one call, one invoice (still the underused moat)

## Part 6: Consistency Audit (Fix Everywhere)

1. **"We" vs "Coastal":** Lead with "we/your" in body copy. Use "Coastal Mobile" only in brand moments (About page, footer, schema).
2. **Phone number format:** Always `813-722-LUBE` in display. `(813) 722-5823` in tel: links and structured data.
3. **Warranty mention:** Every page that lists services must include the 12-month warranty line somewhere (sidebar, footer of section, trust bar). Don't bury it.
4. **Apollo Beach / South Shore:** Mentioned on homepage hero, about, service areas, and in meta descriptions. Not every page.
5. **Service area list (when displayed):** `Apollo Beach · Ruskin · Gibsonton · Riverview · Sun City · Sun City Center · Palmetto · Ellenton` (bullet separators, consistent order).
6. **"Factory-trained" vs "ASE-certified":** Both are true. Use "factory-trained" for consumer pages (warmer), "ASE-certified" in trust bars and fleet/marine contexts (more formal).
